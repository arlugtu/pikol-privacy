Privacy Policy

Pikul — Pickleball Scorekeeping App  |  Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Pikul (“the App”) handles information when you use it on your mobile device. By using Pikul, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Pikul does not require an account, login, or any personal information to function. All game data (scores, player names, session history, settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or files from your device

2. Advertising

Pikul displays ads served by Google AdMob. To serve relevant ads, AdMob may collect and use certain information from your device, including:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address and approximate location (country/region)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions, clicks)
Google AdMob's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy . You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time in your device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party SDKs. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Play Services — policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

All app data — game history, player names, open play sessions, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive). Uninstalling the App permanently deletes this data.

5. Children’s Privacy

Pikul is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
